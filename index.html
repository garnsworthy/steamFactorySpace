The Steam Factory will be able to be reconfigured for a variety of different uses depending on the need at the time. This is necessary given the limited amount of space available for our use and our desire for a broad variety of uses.

We will provide a 24/7 space for makers to create in as well as provide a number of classes for students to develop their skills and learn how to use the space and equipment. A large part of the programming will be geared towards teaching kids software and electronics.

We will provide a space for after school clubs, set designers, robotics teams, lego league and any other group that might need a semi-industrial space.

We are workung to purchase the old Fire Administation Building (summer 2017). The Steam Factory will be located in the basement, accessible via a side door 24/7 for members. We plan to have open house hours frequently throughout the week.

Unfortunately there is no fire pole in the bilding :(
